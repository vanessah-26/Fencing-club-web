Fencing and embrace college experience with joyful activities

UCSC FENCING CLUB is a sport club at the University of California, Santa Cruz. We have fencers in all three weapons: Foil, Saber, and Epee. Besides, we create fun activites outside of fencing to engage our members such as Fencegiving and Graduation party.
